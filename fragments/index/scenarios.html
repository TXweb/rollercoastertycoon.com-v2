ALTON TOWERS by Chris Sawyer & John Wardley

This scenario is based on the United Kingdom's real Alton Towers theme park with the rides it contained at the end of 1999. Not only is it an amazing version of a real-life park, but it also challenges the player with the real difficulties in managing a successful park whilst continuing to grow it. Make sure you check out Chris Sawyer's great versions of Nemesis and Oblivion.

FORT ANACHRONISM by Chris Sawyer

In this scenario, you will need to improve and develop a park which has a fort for its main landmark. Building new rides in this very limited and watery area will definitely challenge a player's skills. This scenario will not work with the demo version of the game.
