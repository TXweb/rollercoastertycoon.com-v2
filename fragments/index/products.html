Pinball Machine:

The ROLLERCOASTER TYCOON pinball machine takes the most exciting elements of the Infogrames' PC game and incorporates them into the fast interactive 3-D world of the pinball playfield. The playfield consists of 4 roller coasters, including "The Flying Ghost," "The Flying Turns," an inverted ramp coaster called "The Chicago Loop," and "The Rocket," a coaster that blasts the ball almost to the playfield glass before sending it on a wild ride around the playfield. Other rides and features include: the "Scrambled Eggs" spinning ride, the pop bumper Bumper Cars, and the hysterical "Dunk the Dummy" side attraction. Designed in the true spirit of the PC game, the ROLLERCOASTER TYCOON pinball machine is a great ride and great fun for everyone.
